Global Game Jam

What?

The global Game Jam is the world's largest game creating event that takes place around the world. There are tens of thousands of participants globally participating virtually and physically.

Where?

The global game jam can be found at its website (globalgamejam.org) or found at a physical location across all 7 continents.

When?

The Global Game Jam occurs once a year beginning at the end of Jan and runs for 6 days.

Why?

The Global Game Jam often has cash prizes from sponsors. One notable example was a $50,000 prize pool in 2022 including a $10,000 first prize. There are also many sponsoring companies that scout for developers. Additionally, placing well is a great indicator of a good game idea and an amazing indicator to companies that you are likely a good candidate.
