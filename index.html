What Is A Game Jam?

Game Jams are timed events in which a team of game developers work together and collaborate to create small, polished games. A game jam can take as long as a couple months or be as short as a couple of hours, within this time many different individuals or teams compete to make the best game. Only two things remain consistent between game jams, every game jam has a time limit and a theme to direct creative design.
